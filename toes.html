TOES

Structure

Toes are bony Digits located at the end of the foot. They wiggle.... voraciously.

Function

Toes serve a critical role in balancing and walking. Thats why we cram them into shoes with narrow toe beds to make their job harder.

Development

If you haven't caught on by now, they also develop in the womb. They grow until they fall off eventually (this is to be avoided if you are still alive).
