KNEES

Structure

Knees are hinges that connect your upper and lower legs. They allow you to do cool things like walk.

Function Knees are the MVP's of your legs. They absorb shock, unless you're me and broke em. Without knees, you'd be like a flamingo -- but less fabulous.

Development

Knees start off as tiny bumps on your fetal legs, and they grow and devlop into big bumps on your adult legs.
